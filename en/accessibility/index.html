Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

INDXONE SASU is committed to making its website accessible to all, in accordance with article 47 of French law No. 201-2016 of October 7, 2016 for a Digital Republic.

Compliance Status

The indxone.com website is partially compliant with the General Accessibility Improvement Framework (RGAA) version 4.1. Non-conformities and exemptions are listed below.

Test Results

The compliance audit conducted on May 17, 2026 shows that ~80% of RGAA criteria are met. Efforts are ongoing to achieve full compliance.

Non-Accessible Content

The following items are not yet compliant:

  • Some color contrasts in dark mode need improvement.
  • Some decorative SVG icons lack explicit aria-hidden="true".
  • Some pages have no breadcrumb navigation.

Planned Improvements

We are actively working to improve accessibility:

  • Adding aria-label to all sections by June 2026.
  • Full axe DevTools audit scheduled for July 2026.
  • Adding breadcrumb navigation to deep pages.
