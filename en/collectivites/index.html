Frequently asked questions

What mayors ask us

Is a town council vote required?
Yes, a simple mayoral decision is sufficient for the entry plan. For higher plans, a council deliberation is recommended. We can help prepare the document.
Will the site really be ready in 48h?
Yes, provided you supply the necessary information quickly (contacts, hours, 1-3 news items, logo if available). The 48 business hour timeline is contractual.
Can we have our own domain name?
Absolutely. Netlify can connect any domain (e.g., mairie-nomcommune.fr) for free. INDXONE handles the DNS configuration. Domain purchase (~€12/year) is the municipality's responsibility.
Are projects eligible for DETR funding?
Yes. Premium Commune and EPCI plans qualify for DETR and DSIL funding. INDXONE assists with the application at no extra cost.
What does native GDPR mean?
The site loads no third-party scripts. No cookie banner is needed. The only data collected is from the contact form — which significantly simplifies your GDPR obligations.
